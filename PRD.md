# Momentum AI

## Product Requirements Document (PRD)

### Tagline

AI-Powered Productivity Companion That Prevents Missed Deadlines

---

# 1. Problem Statement

Students, professionals, entrepreneurs, and teams frequently miss assignments, exams, meetings, interviews, bill payments, project milestones, and important commitments.

Existing productivity tools rely heavily on passive reminders that users often ignore.

Momentum AI moves beyond reminders by proactively helping users plan, prioritize, schedule, and complete tasks before deadlines are missed.

---

# 2. Product Vision

Momentum AI acts as an intelligent productivity partner that continuously analyzes user commitments, predicts risks, and guides users toward timely task completion.

Instead of asking:

"What do I need to do?"

Users can ask:

"What should I do right now?"

and receive personalized recommendations.
